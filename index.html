My Favorite People's

VIRAT KOHLI

Virat Kohli (born November 5, 1988, Delhi, India) is an Indian international cricketer considered by many as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the game. Kohli plays for (and previously captained) the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) in the Indian Premier League (IPL). He is also a former captain of the Indian cricket team in all three international formats—Test cricket, one-day internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 internationals (T20I)—making him one of India’s most successful captains.


ELON MUSK

Elon Musk is a globally recognized entrepreneur and innovator known for his pioneering work in multiple industries. Born in South Africa in 1971, Musk has left an indelible mark on technology, transportation, and space exploration. He co-founded Zip2, played a pivotal role in PayPal, founded SpaceX, revolutionized electric vehicles with Tesla, and promoted sustainable energy through SolarCity. Additionally, he proposed the Hyperloop, founded The Boring Company, created Neuralink, and co-founded OpenAI to advance artificial intelligence research.


LARRY FINK

Larry Fink is the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of BlackRock, the world’s largest asset management firm with over $10 trillion in assets. Born in 1952, he is a highly influential figure in global finance and is known for promoting sustainable and long-term investing through his annual letters to CEOs. Under his leadership, BlackRock has become a major force in shaping corporate behavior, especially in areas like environmental, social, and governance (ESG) practices. While praised for his vision, Fink has also faced criticism for BlackRock’s role in politics and global finance.
